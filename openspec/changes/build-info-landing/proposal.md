## Why

GIBBOR is a groundbreaking technology with complex value propositions spanning blockchain, cryptography, and legal evidence protocols. Without a clear, professional web presence, potential investors, partners, and users cannot understand the problem we're solving or the elegance of our solution. A minimalist, investor-focused landing page communicates our technical rigor and product clarity immediately.

## What Changes

- **New landing page** at the application root (`/`) serving as the primary entry point
- **Hero section** with compelling headline and problem statement
- **Project overview** explaining GIBBOR's value proposition, use cases, and technical foundation
- **How it Works section** describing the workflow (incident trigger → recording → blockchain anchoring)
- **Team section** with organizational information (placeholders for team members)
- **Responsive design** (mobile, tablet, desktop) using minimalist aesthetic
- **No backend required** — static content served via Vite
- **Navigation** throughout page sections with smooth scrolling
